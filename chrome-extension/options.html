SpeedRead Options

Reader and developer preferences

What is this page?

The extension's Options page stores advanced connection settings. You do not need to change them to read. Right-click selected text or a page and choose Speed read this page to open the local reader.

Developer endpoints

Production defaults point at the live SpeedRead app. Change only for local development.

Privacy

Article text is extracted and read locally in the extension. The website opens only when you explicitly choose Open SpeedRead app. License keys stay in extension storage. See privacy policy.
